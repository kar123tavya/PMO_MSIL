Main Dashboard & Gantt

Visualizing your projects in real time.

Overview Dashboard

The Main Dashboard gives you a high-level summary of the entire PMO ecosystem. It displays key performance indicators (KPIs) like Total Projects, Active Projects, and counts for each IL stage.

The Gantt Chart View

By clicking "Gantt View" in the sidebar, you unlock the visual timeline module.

  • Timeline Visualization: Projects are rendered as horizontal bars stretching from their IL1 start date to their IL5 Live date.
  • The "Today" Line: A prominent vertical red line marks today's date so you can instantly see which projects are behind schedule or approaching a deadline.
  • Hover Details: Hovering over any phase inside the Gantt bar will display a tooltip with exact dates and IL metrics.
Exporting the Gantt: You can click the Export button in the top right of the Gantt page to instantly generate an Excel file, a PPTX presentation, or an Image Snapshot of the timeline.
